About Our Company
The company involves a group of students who are inspired to create something that will show and restore Philippines' cultural pride. The company came up with the idea of the name "LocalITy". “LocalITy” means the company was established of local IT students whose goal is to show what local Filipinos have by means of creating a game that will show the Filipino's cultural heritages.
Vision
To be one of the leading technology company that inspires Filipino countrymen through the development of applications and programs. LocalITy aims to build a strong competency among others and to restore cultural heritage of the Philippines.
Mission
LocalITy is an organization that commits itself in providing application solutions towards a better quality of life, in all aspects integrating touches of Philippine culture in creations we make – in order to promote and renovate culture pride amongst the Filipino people.
